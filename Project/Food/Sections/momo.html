Momo Recipe (Veg)

Ingredients

    For Dough:

  • 2 cups maida
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Water (to knead)

    For Filling (Veg):

  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • ½ cup grated carrot
  • 2 tbsp chopped onion
  • 1 tsp garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1–2 green chilies
  • Salt & black pepper
  • 1 tsp soy saucer

    For Red Chutney (Spicy Soul)

  • 3 tomatoes
  • 4–5 dry red chilies
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Little vinegar

Step-by-Step Process

1. Dough Preparation

Mix maida + salt + water…
Knead into a soft dough.
Cover and let it rest for 20–30 minutes.

2. Prepare Filling

In a bowl, mix all filling ingredients.

    Tip:
  • Don’t overcook veggies — raw filling gives better juicy momos
  • For chicken: mix raw keema with spices (it cooks while steaming)

3. Make the Momos Shape

Roll small dough balls into thin circles.
Place filling in center…
Fold and seal (half-moon or pleated shape).

4. Steam Them

Grease steamer.
Place momos carefully.
Steam for 10–12 minutes.

5. Chutney Preparation

Boil tomatoes + red chilies for 5 minutes.
Grind with garlic, salt, vinegar.
