Veg Manchurian (Restaurant Style)

Ingredients

    For Manchurian balls:

  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • ½ cup grated carrot
  • 2 tbsp capsicum (finely chopped)
  • 2–3 tbsp maida (all-purpose flour)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • Salt (to taste)
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • Oil (for frying)

    For sauce:

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 1 tbsp chopped ginger
  • 2 tbsp spring onion
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp chili sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp cornflour (mixed with water)
  • Salt & pepper (to taste)
  • 1 cup water

Steps to Create Magic

1. Prepare the balls

Mix cabbage, carrot, capsicum, salt, pepper, maida, and cornflour.

No extra water—vegetables will release their own moisture.

2. Fry them

Heat oil and deep fry until golden brown and crisp.
Let them rest… like warriors after a fiery battle.

3. Make the sauce

Heat oil → add garlic & ginger → sauté till fragrant.
Add spring onions, then soy sauce, ketchup, chili sauce, vinegar.

4. Thicken & combine

Add cornflour slurry → stir till the sauce becomes glossy.
Now drop the fried balls into the sauce…
Let them soak, absorb, and transform.
