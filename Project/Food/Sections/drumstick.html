Tandoori Chicken Recipe(Home Style Without Tandoor)

Ingredients

    For Chicken:

  • 500g chicken (leg pieces or full cuts)

    First Marination

  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp red chili powder

    Second Marination:

  • 1 cup thick curd (dahi)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder (for color)
  • Salt to taste

Step-by-Step Process

1. Prepare the chicken

Make deep cuts in the chicken pieces… so the spices can seep in like poetry into silence.

2. First Marination

Rub lemon juice, salt, and chili powder all over.
Let it rest for 15–20 minutes.

3. Second Marination

Mix all second marination ingredients into a thick paste.
Coat the chicken fully.

Now comes patience…
Let it rest for at least 4–6 hours (overnight is magic).

Cooking Methods

    Option 1: Oven

  • Preheat to 200oC
  • Place chicken on rack
  • Cook for 25–30 minutes, flipping halfway
