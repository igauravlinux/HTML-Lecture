Jalebi Recipe (Crispy & Juicy)

Ingredients

    For Batter:

  • 1 cup maida
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • ½ cup curd (dahi)
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • Water (to make thick batter)
  • A pinch of turmeric or food color (for that golden glow)

    For Sugar Syrup:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup water
  • 2–3 cardamom (elaichi)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Few strands of saffron (optional)

Step-by-Step Process

1. Prepare the Batter

Mix maida, cornflour, curd, and water to make a smooth, thick batter.
Now let time do its magic…
Cover and rest for 8–10 hours (fermentation).

2. Make Sugar Syrup

Heat sugar + water.
Add cardamom and saffron.
Cook until it becomes 1-string consistency (ek taar ki chashni).
Add lemon juice (prevents crystallization)

3. Fry the Jalebi

  • Heat oil or ghee in a pan
  • Fill batter in a cloth/piping bottle

Now gently… create spirals in hot oil

Dip in Syrup

Immediately dip hot jalebi into warm sugar syrupfor 1–2 minutes.
