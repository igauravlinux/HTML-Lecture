Honey Chilli Potato Recipe

Ingredients

    For Potatoes:

  • 3–4 medium potatoes
  • 3 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp maida (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil (for frying)

    For sauce:

  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp red chili sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp chopped garlic
  • 1 tsp chopped ginger
  • 1–2 green chilies (slit)r
  • 1 small onion (sliced)
  • 1 small capsicum (optional)

    For Garnish:

  • Spring onion
  • Sesame seeds

Preparation

1. Cuts & Soak

Slice potatoes into thin strips (like fries).
Soak in cold water for 15–20 minutes — this removes starch and makes them crisp.

2. Coat the Potatoes

Dry them well.
Mix with cornflour, maida, and salt.
Each piece should wear a light, even coat — like armor before the fire

3. Fry Until Golden

Deep fry (or shallow fry) until crispy and golden.
Keep them aside.

Making the Sauce

4. Build the Flavor

In a pan, heat 1 tbsp oil.
Add garlic, ginger, and green chilies — let them sizzle softly.
Add onions and capsicum, sauté on high flame for that slight crunch.

Create the Magic Sauce

    Add:

  • Soy sauce
  • Chili sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Ketchup
  • Honey
